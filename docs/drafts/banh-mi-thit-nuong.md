## 7. Instructions

### Step 1: Marinate the pork
- **Duration**: `2 hrs (minimum)`
- **Description**: 
  (Combine all marinade ingredients. Coat pork slices evenly. Refrigerate 2 hrs, ideally overnight.)
- **Tip**: (Overnight marinade = deeper flavor + better caramelization.)

### Step 2: Make the pickles
- **Duration**: `30 min`
- **Description**: 
  (Toss carrot + daikon with salt. Rinse, squeeze. Dissolve sugar in vinegar, add veggies. Rest 30 min.)
- **Tip**: (Pickles keep 2 weeks refrigerated — make a big batch.)

### Step 3: Prep the baguettes
- **Duration**: `5 min`
- **Description**: 
  (If bread is soft, toast in oven 200°C for 2-3 min to crisp. Slice lengthwise, don't cut through.)

### Step 4: Grill the pork
- **Duration**: `6-8 min`
- **Description**: 
  (Heat grill pan high. Grill pork 2-3 min each side until caramelized edges. Let rest 2 min before cutting.)
- **Tip**: (Watch for char marks — that's where flavor lives.)

### Step 5: Assemble
- **Duration**: `5 min`
- **Description**: 
  (Spread mayo on one side of baguette, pâté on the other. Layer: pork, pickles, cucumber, cilantro, chilies. Finish with Maggi drops.)

## 9. Variations

- **Bánh mì gà**: Chicken instead of pork — lighter.
- **Bánh mì xíu mại**: Meatballs in tomato sauce — breakfast version.
- **Bánh mì chay**: Vegetarian with tofu + mushrooms.
